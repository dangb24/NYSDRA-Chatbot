Presumptive Alternative Dispute Resolution Program  
 
Erie County Surrogate’s Court is a firm believer in Alternative Dispute Resolution ("ADR") and strongly encourages the use of ADR through the 
following options:   Settlement conferences with the Surrogate and the Surrogate’s Staff , court annexed mediation with a Surrogate Court Roster 
of Neutrals or the loca l Community Dispute Resolution Center ("CDRC").   Mediation and agreed to resolutions can result in enormous savings of 
time and money, reduce stress and create longer lasting agreements that are abided by the participants.   Parties are also encouraged to p ursue 
private resolution options tailored to their needs.  
If you have a matter that is currently pending in Erie County Surrogate’s Court that you would like considered for reference to ADR, or if you 
have a question concerning ADR in Erie County Surrogate ’s Court, please contact the ADR Manager  for Erie County Surrogate’s Court, Rebecca 
Diina LaCivita, Esq., who can be reached by email at rlacivit@nycourts.gov .  For further  Information on Surrogate’s Court visi t: 
http://ww2.nycourts.gov/courts/8jd/erie/surrogates/index.shtml .
