Statewide Mediator Directory

Welcome to the New York State Unified Court System’s Statewide Mediator Directory. This directory lists mediators who are approved to mediate for a trial court in New York State.

Statewide Mediator Directory

Learn more about

selecting a mediator.

Viewing the Directory

The Statewide Mediator Directory is best viewed using the Microsoft Edge or Google Chrome browser.

To view with Internet Explorer, please make sure you are using the most up-to-date version of Internet Explorer, and uncheck “Display intranet sites in Compatibility View” by going to Compatibility View, which is located on the top right of your screen.

ADA & Accessibility

If you have trouble accessing this directory, please email us at

adr-directory@nycourts.gov or call us at 212-428-2760 for assistance in finding a mediator.

For Mediators

Mediators who are currently listed in the directory may update their records using this form or by reaching out to a court staff person who oversees a roster on which they serve.

To apply to become a mediator for a trial court in New York State, please complete our mediator application.
