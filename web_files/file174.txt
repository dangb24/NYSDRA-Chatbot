This site allows NYS attorneys and other authorized persons to file legal papers by electronic means. E-filing
		is authorized for certain case types in certain courts. See our	
		Authorized Courts and Case Types page.

Holiday Closure
		      Courts in E-Filing jurisdictions will be closed on Friday November 10th. 
You may continue to utilize the NYSCEF system during this Holiday closure. Though the NYSCEF Resource Center staff will not be available to respond to emails during this time, you may continue to email your inquiries to the 
NYSCEF Resource Center
 
For emergency Technical issues during weekends, holidays, or after hours contact the Technical Help Desk at 1-800 622-2522.
